Privacy Policy

How PlayBoard handles family and athlete information.

This policy explains what information PlayBoard may collect, how we may use it, and how families can contact us with questions.

Effective date: July 14, 2026

Who this policy applies to

This Privacy Policy applies to PlayBoard websites, forms, emails, communications, and services. In this policy, “PlayBoard,” “we,” “us,” and “our” refer to PlayBoard.

Information we may collect

We may collect information submitted by parents, guardians, athletes, or other users. This may include contact information, athlete information, school and grade information, position, recruiting goals, athletic background, film or profile links, academic information, questions, messages, progress updates, and other information needed to evaluate fit or support the recruiting process.

How we may use information

We may use information to respond to requests, evaluate whether an athlete may be a good fit, provide recruiting guidance, build recruiting plans, support coach outreach, track progress, send follow-up communication, improve PlayBoard, and operate the service.

AI-assisted communication

PlayBoard may use AI-assisted tools to help communicate with users, organize information, draft responses, summarize questions, support recruiting workflows, and improve the experience. We still aim to keep communication practical, useful, and aligned with the recruiting support families expect.

Parent and guardian involvement

PlayBoard is focused on high school football recruiting. If information is submitted for an athlete under 18, parent or guardian involvement is expected. Parents and guardians should review information before it is submitted and contact us with any questions.

Emails and follow-up messages

If you submit information to PlayBoard, we may use your contact information to respond, send follow-up messages, provide program information, and share next steps. You can ask us to stop sending non-essential emails by contacting us.

Third-party tools

PlayBoard may use trusted third-party tools for website hosting, forms, email delivery, database storage, analytics, workflow automation, payment processing, and other business or product operations. These providers may process information only as needed to help us operate PlayBoard.

We do not sell personal information

PlayBoard does not sell personal information. We do not share personal information with recruiters, coaches, schools, advertisers, or other outside parties for their own marketing purposes without permission.

Data security and retention

We use reasonable steps to protect information, but no website, database, or online service can be guaranteed to be completely secure. We keep information for as long as needed to provide PlayBoard, support users, improve the service, comply with obligations, or resolve issues.

Access, updates, and deletion requests

Parents, guardians, athletes, or users can contact us to ask questions, request updates, or request that information be deleted. We may need to verify the request before making changes.

Policy updates

We may update this Privacy Policy as PlayBoard changes. When we update it, we will revise the effective date on this page.

Contact PlayBoard

For privacy questions or requests, contact us at team@playboardfootball.com.
