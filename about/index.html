About PlayBoard

Built for families who need a recruiting plan.

PlayBoard was built for high school football athletes and parents who know recruiting matters, but are not sure what to do next.

Too many athletes are overlooked because they do not have the right guidance, the right school list, the right message, or the right follow-up plan.

Return to homepage

Why it exists

Recruiting should not feel like a guessing game.

Most families are trying to piece the process together from social media, camp flyers, highlight film advice, and random conversations. That creates stress. It also creates missed chances.

Athletes need direction.

They need to know which schools make sense, what to send, and when to follow up.

Parents need clarity.

They need to see the plan without having to manage every step for their athlete.

Coaches need a clean message.

They need quick, useful information that makes it easier to evaluate the athlete.

What shapes PlayBoard

Coach perspective. Parent awareness. Product discipline.

PlayBoard was built from football coaching experience, parent perspective, product and technology work, and recruiting research.

The result is not another profile page. It is a practical system that helps families turn recruiting confusion into a school board, coach-ready emails, follow-up tracking, and weekly next steps.

PlayBoard focus
FitBuild a realistic school-target board.

What we believe

The athlete should not be waiting around.

Recruiting is competitive. Coaches are busy. Families need a plan that is honest, organized, and built around action.

Realistic beats random.The school list should include programs that fit the athlete, not just names everyone already knows.
The athlete has to own it.Parents can support the process, but the player needs to learn how to communicate and follow through.
Follow-up matters.One email is not a recruiting plan. The system has to track what happened and what comes next.
No false promises.PlayBoard does not guarantee offers, scholarships, or coach replies. It gives families a clearer process.
