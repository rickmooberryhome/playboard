Contact / Help

Need help with PlayBoard?

Send us a note and we will point you in the right direction. This page is for families who have questions before starting, athletes working through the profile, and customers who need help after payment.

Email PlayBoard Request information

Best way to reach us

Email is the starting point.

For contact, help, billing, privacy, terms, refunds, or general questions, email us at team@playboardfootball.com.

Before you start

Have questions about whether PlayBoard is a fit? Email us or submit the information request form. We will help you understand the next step before any payment conversation happens.

Athlete profile help

If you are filling out the athlete profile and are not sure what to include, send us a note. We can help you understand what information matters for the recruiting review.

Payment, billing, or refund questions

If you have a Stripe payment, billing, cancellation, or refund question, contact us by email. Include the parent name, athlete name, and the email used for payment so we can find the right record.

Discord access

After payment, Discord is the primary line of communication for ongoing support. If you paid and do not have access, email us and include the email used during signup.

Privacy or data requests

For privacy questions, data updates, or deletion requests, email us at team@playboardfootball.com. We may need to verify the request before making changes.

Recruiting expectations

PlayBoard can help with planning, organization, accountability, and communication support. We do not guarantee offers, scholarships, roster spots, coach replies, or recruiting outcomes.

Not sure where to start?

Start with the information request.

Send the basics and we will follow up with more details about PlayBoard, fit, pricing, and next steps.
