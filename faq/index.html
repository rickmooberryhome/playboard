Basics

What PlayBoard does.

These answers are meant to help families understand who PlayBoard is for, what it can help with, and what it does not promise.

What is PlayBoard?

PlayBoard is a football recruiting mentor and accountability system. It helps athletes understand where they stand, build a realistic school-target board, prepare coach outreach, and stay on track with weekly next steps.

Who is PlayBoard for?

PlayBoard is for high school football players, preferably sophomores, juniors, or seniors, who want to take recruiting seriously but need a clearer plan.

Is this for parents or athletes?

Both. Parents should be informed and able to see progress, but athletes should own and drive the recruiting process. That is what college coaches want to see.

Do you guarantee offers, scholarships, or coach responses?

No. PlayBoard does not guarantee offers, scholarships, roster spots, or coach replies. The goal is to give the athlete a clearer plan, better outreach, stronger follow-up, and a more consistent recruiting process.

Process

How the support works.

What happens after we request information?

You will receive more details about PlayBoard and the next step. If it looks like the athlete may be a good fit, we will explain the program, expectations, and pricing before anything moves forward.

What does the first month focus on?

The first month is about building the baseline. We look at the athlete's profile, film, academics, current outreach, school list, and next best recruiting steps.

What if we do not know what level to target?

That is one of the main reasons PlayBoard exists. The goal is to move from guessing to a realistic school-target board that includes better-fit programs and clear outreach priorities.

What if the athlete already has film, camps, or emails started?

That can help. PlayBoard can organize what already exists, identify what is missing, and make the next steps easier to track.

Pricing + guarantee

Fit comes before pricing.

We are keeping pricing conversations tied to fit. If the athlete may be a good fit for PlayBoard, we will talk through the current program options, pricing, and expectations.

PlayBoard also includes a 30-day money-back guarantee for the first month. If the first month does not give your family a clearer recruiting plan and a better understanding of what needs to happen next, let us know within 30 days and we will refund your first month.

Fit

Who is a good fit?

What makes an athlete a good fit?

A good fit is an athlete who wants to play college football, is willing to take ownership, and is ready to work a consistent plan. Talent matters, but follow-through matters too.

What makes an athlete a poor fit?

PlayBoard is probably not the right fit if the athlete does not want to communicate with coaches, refuses to follow up, or expects recruiting to happen without steady action.
