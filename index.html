The real problem

Most families know recruiting matters. They do not know what to do next.

Random emails, old film, dream-school lists, missed follow-ups, and parent-led communication are not a plan. PlayBoard gives the athlete a clearer path and keeps parents informed without taking over.

How it works

The first month builds the plan. Every month after that works the plan.

01

360 Athlete Review

We review where the athlete stands now: profile, grades, film, Hudl, X account, outreach, target schools, and recruiting gaps.

02

Recruiting Board

We help organize realistic-fit schools, reach schools, coach contacts, follow-ups, and next steps in one clear plan.

03

Weekly Accountability

The athlete gets focused tasks each week. Parents get updates so they can support without running the whole process.

For parents

Support the process without taking it over.

Parents matter. You help organize, ask good questions, and keep your athlete encouraged. PlayBoard gives you visibility while helping your athlete communicate, follow up, and take ownership.

For athletes

College coaches want to see ownership.

PlayBoard helps athletes understand the process, ask better questions, contact the right coaches, and build the habit of doing the next right thing every week.

Membership

Built for families who need a real recruiting system.

Month 1

Build the plan

360 Athlete Review, readiness snapshot, level-fit recommendation, highlight film/Hudl checklist, X account checklist, coach outreach plan, and 30-day action plan.

Ongoing

Work the plan

Weekly recruiting goals, accountability, follow-up reminders, board updates, parent summaries, and help understanding coach responses and next steps.

Time

1 to 5+ hours per week

The workload depends on the time of year, how fast the athlete needs to move, and how much he commits to the process.
