Frequently asked questions

Questions families usually ask first.

Is this only for athletes who already have college interest?

No. PlayBoard can help athletes who already have interest and athletes who are just getting started. The plan changes based on where the athlete stands right now.

Will PlayBoard contact college coaches for my athlete?

No. We help prepare the athlete, build the plan, improve the message, and track follow-ups. The athlete should be the one communicating with coaches.

What if my athlete does not know his GPA, test score, or target schools?

That is normal. PlayBoard explains why the information matters and gives him a clear next step to find it.

How much time does this take each week?

Most athletes should expect 1 to 5+ hours per week depending on the time of year, how much the athlete commits, and how fast he needs to move.
